Priority Scoring

Screenshots are automatically prioritized based on the following criteria:

Urgency Score (60%)

Based on time-sensitive content, such as:

  • Today/tomorrow mentions
  • Dates and deadlines
  • Words like "urgent", "asap", etc.
Actionability (40%)

Based on action-oriented content, such as:

  • Action verbs (apply, submit, call)
  • Phrases like "don't forget to", "need to"
  • Direct instructions

About

Screenshot Triage System is a tool to help you prioritize and manage screenshots containing important information and to-dos.

How it works:
  1. Save screenshots to your designated folders
  2. Click "Scan for New Screenshots" on the home page
  3. The system analyzes the text in each screenshot
  4. Screenshots are prioritized by urgency and actionability
  5. Review your screenshots in order of importance
  6. Dismiss completed items or defer them for later
System Requirement

This system requires Tesseract OCR to be installed on your system for text extraction.

Data Management

Your screenshot data is stored using temporary session storage.
